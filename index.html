About

Jon Ipsaro is a Principal Scientist at Atavistik Bio in Cambridge, Massachusetts. Originally a Cleveland native, he completed his PhD at Northwestern University under Dr. Alfonso Mondragón studying the molecular interactions between spectrin and ankyrin. He then moved to Long Island for post-doctoral research with Dr. Leemor Joshua-Tor at Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory, detailing the molecular underpinnings of RNA interference. His current research leverages integrated biological methods — including biochemistry, biophysics, structural biology, and computational biology — to identify and characterize novel binding sites for small molecule drug targeting.
